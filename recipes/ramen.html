Cheesy Ramen Recipe ^w^!

Description

Melting cheese in ramen makes for an extremely simple and delicious spin on typical ramen noodles that can be easily made in the college environment.

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups of water

  2. 1 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles

  3. 1 slice of american cheese

How to make cheesy ramen noodles

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2: Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes

  3. Step 3: Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packets
