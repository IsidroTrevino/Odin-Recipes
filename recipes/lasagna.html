Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics: If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

Ingredients

  1. Meat:

    2. This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.

  2. Onion and Garlic:

    3. An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.

  3. Tomato products:

    4. You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.

  4. Sugar

    5. Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.

  5. Spice and Seasonings

    6. This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.

  6. Lasagna noodles

    7. Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.

  7. Cheeses

    8. Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.

  8. Egg

    9. An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

How to make lasagna step-by-step

  1. Start by making the sauce: with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes. The three kinds of tomatoes gives the sauce great depth of flavor.

  2. Let this simmer hwile you boil and get the cheeses ready We're using ricotta, shredded mozarella, and parmesan -- like the mix of tomatoes, this 3-cheese blend gives the lasagna great flavor!

  3. From there, its just and assembly job! A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you have three layers and have used up all the ingredients.

  4. Bake until bubbly and ready to eat!
